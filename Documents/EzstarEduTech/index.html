Mathematics: Calculus WAEC Level

Question 1:

Find the derivative of
y = 2x² + 5x

Step-by-Step Solution:

To differentiate y = 2x² + 5x with respect to x, we apply the power rule.

  • The derivative of 2x² is: 2 × (2x^(2-1)) = 4x
  • The derivative of 5x is: 5 × (1x^(1-1)) = 5
dy/dx = 4x + 5
Physics: Mechanics JAMB Level

Question 2:

A car of mass 1000kg accelerates from rest to 20m/s in 10 seconds. What is the net force acting on the car?

Step-by-Step Solution:

Using Newton's Second Law (F = ma) and kinematics (v = u + at):

  • Initial velocity (u) = 0 m/s
  • Final velocity (v) = 20 m/s
  • Time (t) = 10 s
  • Acceleration (a) = (v-u)/t = (20-0)/10 = 2 m/s²
  • Force = Mass × Acceleration = 1000 kg × 2 m/s² = 2000 N
F = 2000 N
